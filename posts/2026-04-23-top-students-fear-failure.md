# Top students often carry too much fear of failure
# 学霸往往最怕失败

## English

One of the biggest problems with top students is that they often carry too much image pressure.

They are used to being seen as capable, disciplined, and exceptional. Because of that, they become more afraid of failure than ordinary people.

So they hesitate.
They do not try easily.
And once they get hit hard, some of them take a very long time to recover.

The irony is that people who are less attached to looking perfect often have an unexpected advantage.

Failure is already familiar to them.
It does not feel like the end of their identity.
So they are less afraid of embarrassment, less afraid of looking foolish, and more willing to try.

That freedom matters.
Because in real life, growth usually does not come from protecting your image.
It comes from experimenting, failing, adjusting, and trying again.

A lot of people are not held back by lack of ability.
They are held back by being too afraid to lose.

## 中文

学霸最大的问题，往往就是偶像包袱太重。

他们太习惯被当成优秀的人、厉害的人、不会出错的人。正因为这样，他们反而比普通人更害怕失败。

所以他们不敢轻易尝试。
一旦摔得重一点，有些人就会很久都站不起来。

反而是那些没那么执着于“必须一直优秀”的人，往往有一种意外的优势。

因为失败对他们来说，本来就是一种常态。
失败不会直接摧毁他们对自己的认同。
所以他们没那么怕丢人，没那么怕出丑，也更敢去试。

而这种自由，其实很重要。
因为现实中的成长，很多时候不是靠维护形象得来的，
而是靠不断尝试，不断失败，不断调整，再继续往前走。

很多人真正的问题，不是能力不够，
而是太想赢，也太怕输。
